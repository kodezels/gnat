Gnat Lalrinchhana

Voice of Mizoram

A beloved Mizo singer whose soulful voice touched the hearts of an entire generation. Gifted not only with music but also an accomplished Muay Thai athlete, Gnat left behind a timeless collection of melodies that continue to resonate across Mizoram.
